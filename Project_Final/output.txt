1,GENERAL PROVISIONS,1,RULES OF CONSTRUCTION,2,"""County"" as including ""parish"", and so forth","The word ""county"" includes a parish, or any other equivalent subdivision of a State or Territory of the United States."
1,GENERAL PROVISIONS,1,RULES OF CONSTRUCTION,3,"""Vessel"" as including all means of water transportation","The word ""vessel"" includes every description of watercraft or other artificial contrivance used, or capable of being used, as a means of transportation on water."
